About This Token

This project was created by Melinna Carsons and a small team of independent developers with a shared interest in exploring how decentralized systems work. The token, titled Tether USD (USDT), is a test and educational asset, designed to mimic the structure and appearance of existing stablecoins for the sole purpose of learning, experimentation, and UI/UX demonstration.

We understand that this token closely resembles the well-known Tether stablecoin in name and appearance — this is intentional, to study how such tokens are integrated across wallets, dApps, and listing platforms. However, this test token is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or backed by Tether Ltd. or any other organization. It holds no real-world value and is not meant for trading or investment.

We’ve also slightly modified the original Tether logo and clearly stated our disclaimer across the site. Our goal is transparency and compliance in the context of blockchain education.

The metadata files, token information, and all relevant project files are available on GitHub under the name Melinna Carsons, and feedback from the developer community is always welcome.
